How to Moonwalk like Michael Jackson

In this page, I will tell you guys how to Moonwalk like Michael Jackson.

You need to know this skill because you can show off this skill infront of people. However, take a risk that your socks and shoes can wear out!

Metarials

  • Slippery floor(If you mastered at moonwalk, then you can try on the tough floor)
  • M.J shoes(for outsise) or socks(for home)
  • If you have, M.J hat and suit(for fancy)

Steps

Step1

Put your right foot behind your left foot and raise your right foot up so that both feet make the L shape.

Step 2

Slide your left foot to the back, and slowly slide upward using your left toe.

Step 3

At the same time, the right foot is placed flat again on the floor. This will cause both feet to take on an L shape again.

Step 4

Cross your feet again in the same way. Just repeat these actions.

After you practiced a lot, finally you can do it smoothly.

More tips
