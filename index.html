Here are the steps

Step1

Place your right foot behind your left foot and raise your right foot up on your right toes so that both feet form to “L” shape.

Step 2

Slide your left foot to the back, and slowly slide upward using your left toe. That means, slide your left foot back and step on it with your left toes.

Step 3

At the same time, the right foot is placed flat again on the sole. This will cause both feet to take on an “L” shape again.

Step 4

Cross your feet again in the same way. Just repeat these actions.
